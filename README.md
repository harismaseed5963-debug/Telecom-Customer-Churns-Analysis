# 📞 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis | Power BI

## 📖 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the biggest challenges in the telecommunications industry. Acquiring new customers is significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones, making customer churn a critical business metric. However, organizations often struggle to identify the factors driving customer attrition due to large volumes of customer data spread across multiple systems.

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze customer churn patterns and uncover the key factors influencing customer retention. By transforming raw customer data into meaningful business insights, the dashboard enables decision-makers to proactively identify high-risk customer segments and implement targeted retention strategies.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Telecom companies lose thousands of customers every year due to service dissatisfaction, pricing concerns, contract preferences, and competition. Without proper analysis, businesses cannot accurately determine:

* Which customers are most likely to churn.
* Which contract types have the highest churn rates.
* How internet services influence customer retention.
* Which payment methods are associated with higher churn.
* The overall financial impact of customer attrition.

Traditional reporting methods often require manual analysis and fail to provide real-time insights for business decision-making.

---

# 💡 Solution

To address these challenges, I developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that centralizes customer data into a single analytical platform.

The dashboard enables users to:

* Monitor overall customer churn performance.
* Compare churn rates across different contract types.
* Analyze churn by internet service category.
* Evaluate customer distribution by gender.
* Identify payment methods associated with higher customer loss.
* Track key business KPIs including Total Customers, Churn Customers, Churn Percentage, Total Revenue, and Average Monthly Charges.

Interactive slicers allow stakeholders to filter the data dynamically and explore customer behavior across multiple dimensions.

---

# 🔍 Key Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer several important business questions, including:

* What percentage of customers have churned?
* Which contract type experiences the highest customer loss?
* Does internet service type influence churn behavior?
* Which payment method has the greatest churn risk?
* How much revenue is associated with the current customer base?
* Which customer segments should be prioritized for retention campaigns?

---

# 📊 Key Insights

The analysis reveals several valuable business insights:

* Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** exhibit significantly higher churn than those on long-term contracts.
* **Fiber Optic** customers experience the highest churn, indicating potential service quality or pricing concerns.
* Customers using **Electronic Check** payments show a greater likelihood of leaving the company.
* Long-term contract customers demonstrate stronger loyalty and lower churn rates.
* Monitoring churn percentage alongside revenue enables management to estimate the financial impact of customer attrition.

---

# 📈 Business Recommendations

Based on the findings, telecom companies can improve customer retention by:

* Encouraging customers to switch from month-to-month to longer-term contracts through attractive incentives.
* Investigating service quality and pricing for Fiber Optic customers.
* Developing targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customer groups.
* Reviewing the customer experience associated with Electronic Check payments.
* Using churn analytics to identify at-risk customers before they leave and take proactive action.

---

# 🛠 Analytical Approach

This project follows an end-to-end Business Intelligence workflow:

1. Data Cleaning and Transformation using Power Query.
2. Data Modeling to establish relationships between customer attributes.
3. Creation of DAX measures to calculate KPIs such as Churn Rate and Revenue.
4. Interactive dashboard development using Power BI visualizations.
5. Business-focused storytelling to support data-driven decision-making.

Rather than simply reporting historical data, this dashboard enables telecom businesses to identify churn patterns, understand customer behavior, and make informed strategic decisions. By highlighting high-risk customer segments and the factors contributing to churn, organizations can improve retention efforts, reduce revenue loss, and enhance customer satisfaction.
